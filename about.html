About Prescott Patriot

In Prescott and the Quad Cities, we take pride in where we live. Even as a newer resident, I noticed that as soon as I came into town, and I love it! Like many others, I used to live in a big city, and didn't know what I was missing until I moved here. Everybody's Hometown teaches everybody a few lessons - about being a friendly neighbor, valuing local businesses, keeping your neighborhood clean and safe, and getting together to walk around town and enjoy local community events. Prescott Patriot is my tribute to the lifestyle and values that make our Prescott area so great.

For now, this is just a simple website and a handful of hats from a local embroidery shop, that I'm trying to find a way to share with neighbors who might want one. Over time, I hope to add more items and graphics celebrating Prescott.
